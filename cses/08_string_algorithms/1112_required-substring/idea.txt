Bai: Required Substring (CSES 1112)

De bai:
Dem so luong xau do dai n tren bang chu cai A-Z (26 ky tu) sao cho xau do
chua mot pattern cho truoc (do dai m) nhu mot substring. In ket qua mod 1e9+7.
Rang buoc: 1 <= n <= 1000, 1 <= m <= 100.

Y tuong chinh: KMP automaton + DP (dem phan bu).
- Dem truc tiep so xau CHUA pattern rat kho vi mot pattern co the xuat hien
  nhieu lan va bi trung lap. Vi vay ta dem so xau KHONG chua pattern, roi lay
  tong tat ca 26^n tru di.
- Xay KMP tren pattern: tinh prefix function (failure function) pi, sau do
  dung no de dung mot automaton (deterministic finite automaton). Trang thai j
  (0 <= j <= m) la do dai cua tien to dai nhat cua pattern dong thoi la hau to
  cua chuoi da doc. aut[j][c] = trang thai ke tiep khi dang o j va doc ky tu c.
  Neu aut[j][c] == m thi pattern vua xuat hien -> xau nay bi loai.
- DP: dp[j] = so xau (voi do dai dang xet) chua BAO GIO cham trang thai m va
  hien dang o trang thai j (0 <= j < m). Khoi tao dp[0] = 1 (xau rong).
  Moi buoc them 1 ky tu: voi moi trang thai j, thu ca 26 ky tu, chuyen sang
  aut[j][c]; chi giu lai cac chuyen tiep co trang thai < m (bo nhung cai dat m
  vi khi do xau da chua pattern). Sau n buoc, tong dp[0..m-1] la so xau KHONG
  chua pattern.
- Ket qua = (26^n - so_xau_khong_chua) mod 1e9+7.

Toi uu: voi moi trang thai j, ta nen (gom) cac ky tu dan toi cung mot trang
thai ke tiep thanh cap (next_state, so_luong), giup vong lap DP nhanh hon.

Tinh dung dan:
- KMP automaton la mot DFA nhan dien "da tung xuat hien pattern". Duong di do
  dai n khong bao gio den trang thai m tuong ung 1-1 voi xau do dai n khong
  chua pattern. Do do phep dem la chinh xac.
- Truong hop m > n: khong the chua pattern, DP se cho tong = 26^n, ket qua = 0.

Do phuc tap:
- Thoi gian: O(n * m * 26) = 1000 * 100 * 26 ~ 2.6 trieu phep -> rat nhanh.
- Bo nho: O(m * 26) cho automaton, O(m) cho DP.
