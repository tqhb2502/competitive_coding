Distinct Routes II - CSES 2130
https://cses.fi/problemset/task/2130

1. Tom tat bai toan
-------------------
Cho do thi CO HUONG voi n phong va m teleporter (canh co huong tu a den b).
Moi teleporter chi duoc dung TOI DA MOT LAN trong toan bo tro choi, va moi lan
dung phai tra 1 coin. Ta phai di tu phong 1 den phong n dung k lan (k ngay).
Vi moi canh dung nhieu nhat mot lan nen k duong di nay la EDGE-DISJOINT (khong
chung canh). Hay tim tong so coin (tong so teleporter su dung) nho nhat, roi in
ra so coin do va k duong di. Neu khong the tao du k duong -> in -1.

Rang buoc: 2 <= n <= 500, 1 <= m <= 1000, 1 <= k <= n-1.

2. Mo hinh Min-Cost Max-Flow (MCMF)
-----------------------------------
Xay dung mang luong (flow network):
  - Moi teleporter a->b tro thanh mot canh co capacity = 1, cost = 1.
    Capacity 1 dam bao moi teleporter dung toi da mot lan; cost 1 dem so coin.
  - source = phong 1, sink = phong n.

Ta can mot luong (flow) co GIA TRI DUNG BANG k voi CHI PHI NHO NHAT. Chi phi
cua luong chinh la tong so canh duoc dung = tong so coin. Neu gia tri luong
cuc dai (max-flow) < k thi khong the tao du k duong di -> dap an -1.

3. Thuat toan: Successive Shortest Paths (SSP)
----------------------------------------------
Lap lai: moi lan tim duong tang luong (augmenting path) NGAN NHAT theo cost
tren do thi thang du (residual), roi bom 1 don vi luong. Vi tat ca cost ban dau
khong am nen dung Dijkstra ket hop Johnson potentials h[] de giu reduced cost
khong am, moi lan Dijkstra chay O(E log V).

  - reduced cost cua canh u->v: c(u,v) + h[u] - h[v] >= 0.
  - sau moi Dijkstra: h[i] += dist[i] (voi cac dinh toi duoc). Khi do h[t]
    chinh la chi phi thuc su cua duong ngan nhat, cong don vao tong cost.

Vi moi capacity = 1, moi lan tang dung 1 don vi luong, nen toi da co k lan lap.
Do phuc tap: O(k * E log V) ~ 500 * 2000 * log(500), rat nhanh so voi gioi han.

4. Phan ra luong thanh k duong di (path decomposition)
------------------------------------------------------
Vi moi cost > 0, luong toi uu KHONG chua chu trinh (mot chu trinh se co tong
cost duong, bo di se giam cost ma van giu gia tri luong -> mau thuan toi uu).
Do do luong la mot DAG-flow phan ra chinh xac thanh k duong di don gian.

Cach cai dat: canh goc thu i (luu tai chi so 2*i trong mang edges) duoc DUNG khi
capacity forward con lai = 0. Voi moi dinh, thu tu tap cac dinh ke ra tu cac
canh da dung: nxt[u]. Sau do lap k lan: bat dau tu phong 1, moi buoc lay mot
canh ra chua dung (dung con tro ptr[u]) di tiep cho den khi toi phong n.

Tinh dung: tai moi dinh trong (khac 1 va n), so canh vao da dung = so canh ra
da dung (bao toan luong). Moi lan "den" mot dinh se tieu thu dung mot canh ra,
nen luon con canh ra de di tiep, va khong bao gio vuot qua so canh. Phong 1 co
dung k canh ra (bat dau k duong), phong n co dung k canh vao (ket thuc k duong).

5. In ket qua
-------------
  - Dong 1: tong so coin (= cost = tong so teleporter da dung).
  - Voi moi trong k duong di: mot dong ghi SO PHONG tren duong, roi mot dong
    liet ke cac phong theo thu tu tu 1 den n.

6. Do phuc tap
--------------
  - Thoi gian: O(k * E log V), voi E = O(m), V = n. Rat du nhanh cho n<=500,
    m<=1000, k<=n-1.
  - Bo nho: O(n + m) cho do thi va cac mang phu tro.
