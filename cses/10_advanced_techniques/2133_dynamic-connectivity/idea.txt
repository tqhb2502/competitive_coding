Dynamic Connectivity - CSES 2133

BAI TOAN
--------
Cho do thi vo huong voi n dinh va m canh ban dau. Sau do co k su kien, moi su
kien la them mot canh (loai 1) hoac xoa mot canh (loai 2). Ta phai in ra k+1 so:
so thanh phan lien thong cua do thi ban dau (truoc su kien dau tien), va sau moi
su kien tiep theo.

Rang buoc: 2 <= n <= 1e5; 1 <= m, k <= 1e5.

Y TUONG CHINH: offline dynamic connectivity
--------------------------------------------
Bai toan them/xoa canh khong the giai truc tiep bang DSU thong thuong vi DSU
khong ho tro thao tac xoa (undo). Ky thuat chuan la:

  segment tree tren truc thoi gian + DSU-with-rollback (union by size, KHONG
  path compression).

Coi cac "trang thai" la 0, 1, ..., k (trang thai 0 la do thi ban dau, trang thai
t la sau khi ap dung t su kien dau tien). Moi canh cu the ton tai trong mot (hoac
nhieu) khoang lien tiep cac trang thai:

  - Canh ban dau: bat dau ton tai tu trang thai 0.
  - Canh them boi su kien i (loai 1): bat dau ton tai tu trang thai i.
  - Canh xoa boi su kien i (loai 2): ton tai trong khoang [start, i-1], voi start
    la thoi diem no duoc them vao (hoac 0 neu la canh ban dau).
  - Canh khong bao gio bi xoa: ton tai den tan trang thai k, khoang [start, k].

Ta dung mot unordered_map tu ma hoa cap dinh (min*BASE + max) sang mot stack cac
thoi diem start dang "mo". Khi xoa canh, ta lay start o dinh stack ra va sinh mot
khoang [start, i-1]. Cach dung stack xu ly dung ca truong hop co nhieu canh trung
lap giua cung mot cap dinh (multi-edge), vi moi ban sao co tuoi tho rieng.

SEGMENT TREE TREN THOI GIAN
---------------------------
Xay segment tree tren mien [0, k] (k+1 la). Voi moi (canh, khoang [l, r]), ta them
canh do vao O(log k) node phu khoang [l, r] (giong cach cap nhat "lazy" phu doan).
Tong so ban ghi la O((m + k) log k), vua du bo nho.

Sau do DFS segment tree:
  - Tai moi node: ghi lai checkpoint = kich thuoc stack rollback, roi union tat ca
    cac canh luu tai node do.
  - Neu la node la (nl == nr): so thanh phan lien thong cua trang thai nl chinh la
    dsu.comp hien tai.
  - Neu khong phai la: de quy xuong hai con.
  - Truoc khi tra ve: rollback tat ca cac union da thuc hien tai node nay ve
    checkpoint.

DSU-with-rollback
-----------------
Dung union by size de cay find co do cao O(log n), va KHONG dung path compression
(vi path compression khong the undo mot cach re tien). Moi lan union thuc su lam
thay doi cau truc, ta luu (root, child) vao stack ops va giam bien dem comp. Khi
rollback, ta bat cac ban ghi ra khoi stack, khoi phuc par[child] = child, tru lai
sz[root], va tang comp. So thanh phan lien thong luon bang comp = n - (so union
thanh cong tren duong tu goc xuong node hien tai).

TINH DUNG DAN
-------------
Tai moi trang thai t, tap canh dang "song" la dung tap canh ma cac khoang ton tai
cua chung chua t. Trong qua trinh DFS, tren duong tu goc den la t, cac node ma ta
di qua chinh la cac node ma segment tree dung de bieu dien tap khoang chua t; do
do tat ca (va chi) cac canh song tai t da duoc union khi ta cham la t. Vi vay
dsu.comp tai la t la so thanh phan lien thong dung cua trang thai t.

DO PHUC TAP
-----------
- Thoi gian: O((m + k) log k * alpha) ~ O((m + k) log k * log n). Moi canh xuat
  hien trong O(log k) node, moi union/find ton O(log n). Voi n, m, k = 1e5 chay
  rat nhanh trong gioi han thoi gian cua CSES.
- Bo nho: O((m + k) log k) cho cac ban ghi canh trong segment tree, cong O(n) cho
  DSU va O(k) cho mang ket qua.
